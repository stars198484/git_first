In fact, all of these periods pale when compared to London as it is today. Not only is London the greatest, most dynamic city in the world, it’s never been better than it is now.
Comparison may be the thief of joy, and it might be invidious to square London off against other fashionable cities such as New York, Milan or Paris – which is heavier, a tonne of feathers of a tonne of gold? – but right now, there is no other city in the world like it.
